Proximity

Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org/

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints really uses the principle of proximity for their webesite. They place articles and talks next to each other that all relate to Jesus Christ. They use proximity to teach people that the purpose of the church is to bring people close to Christ.

Contrast

Verizon

verizon.com

Verizon uses the principle contrast to emphasize what they have to offer. You can see that the background is a pitch black with white lettering. This helps users focus go right where Verizon wants to. With contrast they say their message loud and clear and make an instant impact on the reader.

Allignment

Apple

apple.com

The apple website has a very sleek design that uses allignment very effectively. The website has everything down to headings,images, and links alligned making the website very pleasing to look at. Another thing they do very well that is not mentioned above is the user interface.
